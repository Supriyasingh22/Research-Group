About Us

Machine Learning Research Group (MLRG) can be broadly categorized into Theoretical analysis and applications of Machine Learning. MLRG's research interests span a wide range of topics in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. Model-based sentiment analysis, named entity recognition, entity linking and coreference resolution and product attribute extraction, Topic Modeling, Applications of Machine Learning to Information Retrieval, Document similarity and classification, Natural Language Processing, ML for Healthcare, ML for Social Good.
